Wells-Lamson Quarry Artist Studio

East Barre, Vermont
A necklace of artist studio pods was hung off of the perpendicular face of the Wells-Lamson Quarry with a choreographed circulation from the brim down to the water’s edge. There were a plethora of challenges that were resolved through research and testing adhesives, building techniques, and the consultation of a structural engineer.

The driveway is the connection between the farm and the community

First, the program was injected into the driveway

Then the massing was integrated into the site’s topography
