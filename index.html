Sustainable House

Designed for sustainablity, this house is situated on a plot of land in the sand hills of Wisconsin. It has a facade of quarried limestone from Wisconsin and thick insulated walls. An asterisk-shaped form allows for the rooms to direct views out towards picturesque vistas.
